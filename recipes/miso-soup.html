Traditional Miso Soup

Have you ever wanted to Japan to try traditional Miso Soup? Well now you can eat it from the comfort of your own home! This recipe was compiled after I visited Japan and extensively tried miso soup across the country.

Follow these steps and you will have miso soup ready in 30 minutes

Quick and easy and ready in 30 minutes!

This is freezable as well so you can have it ready for a rainy day!

Ingredients:

  • 50 grams Miso Paste
  • 400 ml Water
  • 1 bunch Spring Onions

Steps:

  1. Boil the water in a kettle
  2. Add the boiling to the water in a pan with the miso paste
  3. Meanwhile, finely chop the spring onions
  4. When the miso is compeltely dissolved, add the broth to a bowl
  5. Garnish the broth with the spring onions
  6. Enjoy!
