Lasagne Recipe

The best lasagna you'll ever make!

Ever remember having lasagna as a kid when your parent would make it? This is the recipe for you as it will bring you back to that nostalgic feeling!

Made with the finest ingredients, this lasagna will be sure to impress at your next dinner. What's better, you can vary the ingredients accordingly to make a meal for 8 people!

Be sure to read the recipe first to ensure you have all the ingredients ready. This recipe should only take 1 hour and can be prepared well ahead of the meal, allowing you plenty of time to mingle with guests and friends!

Ingredients:

  • 8 Lasagna sheets
  • 400 grams Canned Tomatoes
  • tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp High Quality Oil
  • 400 grams Mince Beef
  • 200 ml Milk
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Steps:

  1. Combine the flour, butter, and milk to make a bechamel
  2. In a separate pan, cook the mince on high heat in the oil
  3. Remove the beef once cooked, add the onions and garlic and saute
  4. Add the beef back to the pan and add the tomato paste with the canned tomatoes
  5. Layer a baking dish with beef, pasta sheets and tomato sauce, and bechamel until all is in the pan
  6. Bake the dish for 1 hour at 220 celcius
  7. Enjoy!
